WHAT IS THIS APP?
-----------------
AI Expense Tracker is a smart money management tool. Before you spend money
on anything, you describe the expense to the app and the AI tells you whether
it's a good idea based on your budget. It also tracks everything you've spent
so you can see where your money goes each month.

Think of it as a financial advisor in your pocket — always available, always
honest, and always based on your actual numbers.


WHO IS IT FOR?
--------------
There are two types of accounts:

  USERS — That's you. You register, set your income and budget goals, add
  expenses, and get AI advice before spending.

  ADMIN — The system manager. They can only view platform-wide statistics.
  They cannot add expenses or use the app like you do.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GETTING STARTED
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

STEP 1 — Open the app
  Go to: http://localhost:5173
  You'll see the homepage. Click "Get Started" or "Register".

STEP 2 — Create your account (3 steps)

  Step 1 of 3 — Basic info
    Enter your name, email, and a password (minimum 6 characters).

  Step 2 of 3 — Your income
    Enter your monthly take-home income.
    Example: if you earn ₹50,000 per month, enter 50000.

  Step 3 of 3 — Budget goals
    Use the sliders to decide how to split your income:

    🟢 Productive (recommended: 60%)
       Essential and growth-oriented spending.
       Examples: rent, groceries, electricity, education, healthcare.

    🔴 Unproductive (recommended: 20%)
       Fun and discretionary spending.
       Examples: movies, dining out, shopping, gaming.

    🔵 Savings (recommended: 20%)
       Money set aside for the future.
       Examples: emergency fund, investments, fixed deposits.

    The three sliders must always add up to 100%.
    They auto-adjust when you move one.

  Click "Complete Registration" — you're logged in automatically.

DAILY USE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ADDING AN EXPENSE
-----------------
This is the core feature. Use it before or right after spending.

1. Click "Add Expense" from the dashboard or menu.

2. Fill in four fields:

   Title    — What is the expense?
              Example: "New headphones", "Electricity bill", "Online course"

   Amount   — How much does it cost?
              Example: 3500

   Category — Pick the closest match from the dropdown.
              Options: Food & Dining, Transportation, Shopping, Entertainment,
              Bills & Utilities, Healthcare, Education, Travel, Other.

   Purpose  — Why are you spending this money?
              Be specific. The AI uses this to understand your expense.
              Example: "Monthly electricity bill for home"
              Not helpful: "just need it"

3. Click "Get AI Suggestion".

4. The AI will analyze your expense and show you:

   🏷️  Category — What type of expense the AI thinks this is
       (Productive / Unproductive / Savings)
       You don't need to decide this yourself — the AI figures it out.

   ✅ or ❌  Decision — Should you spend this money?
       YES = fits your budget, go ahead
       NO  = exceeds your budget or not recommended right now

   📝  Reason — Why the AI made that decision.
       It explains the budget situation clearly.

   💡  Suggestion — If the AI says NO, it offers alternatives.
       Example: "Consider waiting until next month" or "Look for a cheaper option."

   📊  Budget Impact — Numbers showing:
       - How much you've already spent in that category
       - Your budget limit for that category
       - What your total would be after this expense
       - What percentage of your budget this uses

5. Read the AI's analysis carefully.

6. Decide:
   - AI says YES → click "Add Expense" to save it.
   - AI says NO  → you can still add it if you choose, but the AI is warning you.
     The final decision is always yours.


VIEWING YOUR EXPENSES
---------------------
Click "Expenses" in the menu to see your full expense history.

Each expense shows:
  - Title and amount
  - Category and date
  - Type (Productive / Unproductive / Savings) — set by the AI
  - AI decision (Recommended / Not Recommended)
  - AI reason


UPDATING YOUR BUDGET
--------------------
Click "Budget Settings" to:
  - Update your monthly income (e.g. after a salary change)
  - Adjust your budget ratios using sliders

Changes take effect immediately for future AI suggestions.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW THE AI WORKS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The AI is powered by LLaMA 3.3 70B (via Groq). It receives:
  - Your monthly income
  - Your budget ratios and limits
  - How much you've already spent this month in each category
  - The expense details you entered (title, amount, category, purpose)

It then decides:
  1. What type of expense this is (productive / unproductive / savings)
  2. Whether it fits within your budget
  3. Whether to recommend it (YES or NO)
  4. A clear explanation and suggestion

The AI does NOT store your data or learn between sessions. Every suggestion
is based on the information you provide at that moment.

IMPORTANT: The AI gives advice. You make the final decision.

TIPS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DO:
  ✓ Add expenses as soon as you spend — don't let them pile up
  ✓ Write a clear, specific purpose — the AI uses it to give better advice
  ✓ Check your dashboard regularly to stay aware of your spending
  ✓ Update your income in Budget Settings if it changes
  ✓ Read the AI's reason even when it says YES — it's informative

DON'T:
  ✗ Write vague purposes like "misc" or "stuff" — the AI can't help properly
  ✗ Ignore AI warnings — they're based on your real numbers
  ✗ Set unrealistic budget ratios — be honest with yourself
  ✗ Forget to update your income after a raise or pay cut

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q: Do I need to know if my expense is productive or unproductive?
A: No. The AI categorizes it automatically based on what you describe.

Q: What if I disagree with the AI's category?
A: You can still add the expense. The AI advises — you decide.
   But consider why it categorized it that way before overriding.

Q: What if AI says NO but I still want to spend?
A: You can add it anyway. The AI is a guide, not a gatekeeper.
   Just be aware of the budget impact it showed you.

Q: Can I change my budget ratios later?
A: Yes. Go to Budget Settings anytime and adjust the sliders.

Q: Can I delete an expense I added by mistake?
A: Yes. Go to the Expenses page and use the delete option.

Q: How often should I use the app?
A: Ideally every time you spend something. The more consistent you are,
   the more accurate your budget picture will be.

Q: Can I use this on my phone?
A: Yes. The app is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Q: Is my data private?
A: Yes. Only you can see your expenses. The admin can only see platform-wide
   statistics — not your individual expense details.

Q: What if I forget my password?
A: Contact the system administrator to reset it.
